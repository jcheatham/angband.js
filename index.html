Welcome to J/ADORE - Javascript/Ajax Driven Online Roguelike Experiment! Hopefully that's quite self explanatory. If you're new to Roguelikes, I recommend you check out The Wikipedia Article for a basic explanation. J/ADORE is mostly inspired by the roguelike "Angband" and variants. At the moment, J/ADORE is in very early stages (pre-alpha?), and as such lack most features and is unplayable as a game. But, I think it's already an important proof-of-concept. Despite the name, it's currently lacking any Ajax, but the plan is to have some as a method of saving and uploading scores at some point.
